MSH Healthcare is a medical-affairs group founded in Shanghai in 2013 and listed on the Main Board of The Stock Exchange of Hong Kong (HKEX: 2415) in 2024. The Group provides integrated clinical, scientific, and market-access services across the China–U.S. corridor, with a disciplined operating model, audited reporting under IFRS, and a physician-in-the-loop compliance framework across every AI-assisted workstream.

We do not position ourselves as a technology vendor. We are a medical-affairs operator that uses technology — deliberately, and with accountability — in service of pharmaceutical and device sponsors entering new geographies.
