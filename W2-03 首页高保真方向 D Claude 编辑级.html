N° 04 · AI & Physician Network

AI that goes through a physician — always.

We call this PITL · Physician-in-the-Loop. No claim leaves the studio without a named specialist's sign-off, and every publish is blocked until each quantified claim is bound to a verified proof-point.

PITL v1.0

Disclosure. AI drafts and retrieves; a named specialist signs. Review cadence ≤ 180 days; last reviewed 2026-04-02 by Legal + Compliance. Model inventory is on our AI page; customer-bound models are not.
